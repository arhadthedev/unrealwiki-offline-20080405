Description

WarPath is a budget-priced first-person shooter computer game developed by Digital Extremes, who previously developed Pariah. The game was originally being developed as a sequel to Pariah, but since that game was a commercial flop, WarPath continued development as a whole new game. However, the similarities between the gameplay of both games are very evident.
