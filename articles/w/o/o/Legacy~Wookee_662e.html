Legacy:Wookee

From Unreal Wiki, The Unreal Engine Documentation Site

Wookee, an object-oriented, modular, extensible formatter for Wiki markup created by Mychaeel in Wikipedia:Perl. Designed to replace the built-in formatter of the UseMod:UseModWiki script the Unreal Wiki is using.

  • Legacy:Wookee/Installation – How to get Wookee into UseMod Wiki
  • Legacy:Wookee/Changes – What's new in Wookee?
  • Legacy:Wookee/Problems – Something not working as expected?
  • Legacy:Wookee/Suggestions – Suggestions for Wookee are discussed here and on the forum
  • Legacy:Wookee/CSS – Documents all CSS attributes used in Wookee-generated HTML
  • Legacy:Wookee/Classes – Documents Wookee's class structure

You can download the Wookee-related modules from http://mb.link-m.de/wiki/Wookee.zip. The download does not include the Unreal Wiki-specific stuff, including the image markup – it's just too far integrated into our local Wiki installation to be portable without much hassle.
