about me:

i am one of the older mappers here having started on UED2.0 for ut99, i made a wide range of map for the strangelove mutator and a few weird maps i carried on with UED2.0 until last year when i found XMP and made a couple of simple maps as well as xmp-CXP-kilovu and xmp-CXP-valleyofthedead and a few others for the cXp. As i only had experiance with UED2.0 all the new features included in this editor were fairly new (i had a mess about in ut2003 editor before xmp came out)so i had to learn new skills, kilovu is probebly the best that i have made so far as the FPS is above playable even on slower computers its a smallish map very open (countered with decent fog) and the terrain is great for walljumping i also messed with sounds and ogt a great atmoshphere
