Rant1: "OldSkool"

Well, with UEngine3 coming soon, and more and more 'profesional' mod-teams appearing for UT2k4, i can't help but glace back at my monster 10G UnrealTournament folder and wonder: "Why the heck do I find myself playing ONP, SevenB, and Unreal1 over and over again? And then I answer myself; it's because:

  1. They are WAY more fun than RedOrchestra or AlienSwarm...or CarBall,...or any of those 'modernprofesional' mods that keep on appearing for UT2k4...
  2. Graphics. I like the 'sucky' [WetTextures] WAY more than [FluidSurfaceInfo]s; at high res and color they get a never-ending infinate detain, just zoom into a puddle at 1280*1024 32 bit to see what i mean...and the texturing is way...cooler...more 'sci-fi' than UT2k4...
  3. Build. The architecture looks...so...smoooth...so much better than any LM'ed mesh can (lightmapped). Everything was soo....dynamic...it was beautiful just to look at...
  4. Standards. If you make a map for U2k4, then it'll be rated a 0 in no time if you dont have:
    • Custom meshes
    • Custom Scripts
    • Custom Sounds
    • Custom Textures
    • Custom Music

If you don't have any of the above, its a newb map. For UT99, however, all you need is a :

Complex Mover Brush that you made yourself.

THATS IT! JUST A MOVER!!!! THEY WERE SOOO HARD AND SOOO FUN TO MAKE THAT THEY WERE THE STANDARD! :-(

also, ut2k4 has NO singleplayer, NO coop, NO INVENTORY SYSTEM, the weapons take 6 HOURS TO MAKE, and whereas in ut99, it just took 15 mins....

ok im done here, i have recently dedicated myself to modding for OldSkool Amp'd , i hope others follow in my footsteps...

Plz comment ...here.
