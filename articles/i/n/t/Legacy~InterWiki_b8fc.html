Legacy:InterWiki

From Unreal Wiki, The Unreal Engine Documentation Site

Interwiki is a special Wiki Markup for creating links to other Wiki sites. (See MeatBall:InterWiki for more on this concept.) In general, links like this:

 [[SiteName:RemotePage]]

Note that the prefixes are case-sensitive.
