Me

To start off with i'm completely new to unreal scripting

I've done my fair share of c++, java, python, etc. but i've never really made a game beyond simple tetris (a gruelling school project in c++ that was completely annoying). However a group of my friends and I have been planning a mod for sometime and as soon as we got a hold of UT2003 it just clicked and we began work.

In terms of who I am, I'm currently in high school and planning on goint to MIT for college. My school offers zero in the way of useful tech courses so i've been stuck learning everything on my own.

Criminal Uprising

Criminal Uprising is the total conversion for UT2003 mod that i'm currently working on. It will be team based combat with real world weapons, etc. It will entail compltely overhauling unreal with new weapons, models, animations, physics, etc. At this time there we have no site online, but have models and preliminary coding done, not to mention about 100+ pages of typed research and goals.

Why This DevPage is Here

Basically as we get more coding done I will post it up here. I will also try and post any aspects of making this mod that do not already exist on Wiki. Like I said i'm new to unreal script, but this game looks like a very interesting and promising challenge that i look forward too.
