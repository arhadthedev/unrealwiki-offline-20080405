Legacy:Surface

From Unreal Wiki, The Unreal Engine Documentation Site

A surface in UnrealEd is a partial plane in a 3D viewport, that can be selected with a single click. The term is sometimes confused with a Texture (UT), which is the image applied to a surface.

The polycount is the number of surfaces, either currently visible or in the map as a whole.

See Surface Properties window and Surface Flags (UT) for the settings that can be applied to surfaces in UnrealEd.
