General Info

Not a full TC, this is a collection of player models, weapons, maps, and vehicles based on the popular webcomic Megatokyo The primary reason I am creating this mod is to practice multiple aspects of mod creation and to use this as an example to developers of what I can do.

Current Progress

Currently I only have a few things ingame and mostly working:

  • Player Models
    • Piroko
    • Miho
    • SGD
  • Weapons
    • Killstick

I am currently working on getting the next weapon started, dual shotguns, and am recoding the killstick for UT2004.

For more info and downloads of what I have completed see my webpage
