Work Im doing

Mods

Well the Triggerman mod I was working on has kinda been put on the back burner since I couldnt pull a decent team together, the team members I did have all but dissapeared now and my forum host went by by.
I decided to join a mod called UC2004 (http://www.UC2004.com/) which is a remake of Unreal Championship 2 for PC using the UT2004 engine. We are making alot of progress in the short amount of time I have been on the team, I am currently a coder/modeller for the mod team which is called Total Adrenaline Productions. Im really happy about this as the team is skilled and are very motivated, so it looks to be a good ride. We are however looking for coders and animators so if you like this idea and want to give us a hand drop by: UC2004 Forums

Skillz

Modelling

Ive been modelling in max since version 2.5, developed a small menu plugin called Quadmx7 which isnt publicly released.

Graphics

I learnt the Adobe Suite while doing a few TAFE courses, Photoshop and Illustrator are my strong points, I can use Premiere and Indesign also. Ive learnt alot more since doing these courses and am always looking to expand my skills. The Visual Arts and Technology course I did covered alot of stuff though not related directly to technology like design fundamentals, drawing foundations and design visualisation. There was some art critique involved but I really didnt like that as I prefer to do critiques on stuff where my knowledge is best, namely the interactive entertainment industry.

UnrealScript

For the last few years and I dabble in unreal scripting, Im really happy at where this is going and thanks to you guys Im 10x better than when I started.

Conclusion

My knowledge of games and game design is quite large but theres always more to learn. Thats what really attracted me to the wiki, this is a great place for people of all skills and skill levels to come, learn and contribute. I hope to add a little to the wiki myself and as time goes by, I hope to improve on my skills as well as help others improve on thiers.

Anyways you've probably heard enough by now.

Off you go do some modding! ;)
