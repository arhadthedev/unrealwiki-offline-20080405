If you ended up here you must hve clicked on my name somewhere.

So, it seems right I give you a bit more information on myself.

My actual name is Francesco Rizzi, but I also go by Frank, which seems easier for many Americans.

Online, I often use the nick Olorin as you might have guessed. For those of you wondering where that comes from, I can only point you to The Lord of the Rings by Tolkien.

I was born in Italy, and moved to the United States for college, in Georgia. I now live in Florida, and work as a programmer.

I have been a judge at the Southeastern Regional ACM Programming Competition for 2 years now, so, if you're involved with that, drop a line ! I am very interested in hearing feedback about our past competitions!

If you need to get in touch with me, feel free to use my francesco.rizzi@gmail.com address spam will simply be deleted :hmm:

In my spare time, I play Unreal Tournament 2004, and I am working on my very first map for it.

That should give you a better idea.
