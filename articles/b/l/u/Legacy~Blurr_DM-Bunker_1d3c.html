The Map

This map consists of 3 rooms, connected in a triangle-like fasion. The Map supports 11 players. The map may not look too good, but my friends and I think that it's really fun to play. You won't go to far without running into someone.

History

This is a map that I put together over two weeks. I'm looking for somone to do some static meshes for the map. If you think you can help, e-mail me at alex@blisserv.net. I haven't added full bot support for it yet. (Bots move strangely)
