Total Destruction

The map I had been working on has totally destroyed itself in one of the weirdest glitches in the history of mankind. I turned off the computer, and then the next day turned it back on, as I do everyday. I proceeded to attempt to open the map from the map folder, and saw nothing. NOTHING. Every map had been deleted. I decided, "Well, I have some backups, I'll just get one of those." Those were gone too. I proceeded to search the entire hard drive for any Unreal Tournament 2004 map files to find nothing. The computer had taken it upon itself to delete every last map file from the computer, and the only one remaining was one I had on my flashdrive, and that one was a few months old, making it totally pointless to attempt to make up that lost time.
