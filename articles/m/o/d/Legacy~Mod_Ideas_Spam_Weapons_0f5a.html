Mod ideas for UT 2003 – Spam Weapons

Description

All weapons are replaced with a spammer's weapons of choice like the Ripper, the UT Flak Cannon (with its ever bouncing shapnel) and others.

And here is the problem: All those weapons can be dangerous to their owner. And another problem: You can't really stop firing. You have to fire at least once every three seconds or your weapon will fire on its own. To make things worse suicides will cause you to loose 10 points → not very desirable in a death match.

Interested Scripters

If you are interested in developing this mod for UT2003 then add your name to the list. Once you start development you should indicate that below (and hopefully include a link to a journal page). Before you start development you should also check this section to see if anyone else has started.
