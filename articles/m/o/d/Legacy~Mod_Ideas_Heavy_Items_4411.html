Description

This mutator reduces the speed players can move (and the height they can jump) by examining the players inventory for weapons and objects. The rules are as follows:

  • For each weapon the player is carrying the player's speed is reduced by 1%.
  • For each full amount of ammo the player's speed is reduced by 1% (in 0.1% increments).
  • Each item carried (flag, ball, etc) reduced the player's speed by 1%.
