Mod ideas for UT 2003 – Sprint for Points

Description

This mod is designed to be played on Domination maps. In order to score a points for your team you must touch two control points consecutively without being killed. The number of points your team gets increases as more and more control points are touched consecutively. So, to get 1 point for your team you must touch one control point and then touch the other without dying. If you manage this then you can run back and touch the other control point to gain more points. Touching the same control point more than once doesn't count. The scor your team gets when you touch a control point in a sequence is calculated as 2 ^ (n-2) squared where n is the number of consecutive control points touched.
