Description

Just finished playing some 2K3 and got a crazy idea. What if we could have a view from out of a helmet?

I'd say that would be neat. Would make things a little different.

Also I could even have filters to say detect movement or infa-red. Or have a light switch. So many ideas.

If you've ever played aliens vs. predator there are alot of filters that are in use in that game.

Could possibly use some of the same things.

Maybe even a new hud, or targeting systems on the hud. That would be mint, like a grid or auto zoom onto enemys and stuff. That would be pro. Auto targeting, lasers and stuff. I think we need a mirror to see behind you too, why not make one of them, or maybe make it a camera so that it looks a bit grainy like how the opponents faces show up on the side. Ya that would be pro.

Features

  • A View Through A Helmet
  • Infa-Red
  • Light Perhaps?
  • Would Make Gameplay More Cyberish
  • New Hud
  • Grid Lines
  • Auto Zoom
  • Lasers
  • Auto Targeting (Or Targeting Help Depending On Your Weapon)
  • Camera To Look Behind
