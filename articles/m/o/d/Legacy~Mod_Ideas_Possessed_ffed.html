Mod ideas for UT 2003 – Possessed.

Description

This mod is an unusual take on a standard deathmatch. Players spawn as ghosts and must zoom around the level attempting to find a body to inhabit. Once in a body, the fragging can begin. The player that stays within a body for the longest amount of time wins the game.

While in "ghost mode" a player will continually lose points at the rate of 1 point per half second. In ghost mode players cannot collect objects although they can trigger doors and switches and travel through teleporters. Upon collision with a body a player in ghost mode will enter the body and be able to play normally. Ghost mode does not allow players to walk through walls and the like – the level must be traversed normally. Ghosts move 25% faster than normal players.

While inhabiting a body players interact with the objects and levels as they would normally. If a player is killed they immediately become a ghost. If the body was not gibbed then it simply lies on the floor awaiting a new inhabitant. If it was gibbed then a new body is spawned somewhere around the level. Players cannot enter the body they last inhabited.

Bodies are initially spawned randomly around the level. There are always the same number of bodies available as there are players.
