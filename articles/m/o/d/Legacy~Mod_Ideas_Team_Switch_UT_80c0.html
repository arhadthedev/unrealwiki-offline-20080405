Team Switch (UT)

Basicaly this works like the DOM control point. The trigger has 4 different outevents, one for each team.

I thought it may be usefull in domination but could be used for anything really.

Here goes

The trigger will change its outevent based on the team color of the last player to activate it.

So you could place it next to a DOM control point, which ever team is in possesion of the CP will also be in control of the trigger. Each CP has a TeamSwitch, linked up to a door or bridge or something to another area.

You have to get all of TeamSwith to your team color before the door will open.
