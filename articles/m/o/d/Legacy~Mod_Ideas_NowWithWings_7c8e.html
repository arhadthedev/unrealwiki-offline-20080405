Description

When I came up with this one (1am this morning while failing to get to sleep) I thought it ought to be called Faeries. When I woke up this morning I decided that maybe Faeries wasn't such a good name after all.

OK, This mod provides players with two ways of getting around. One is the usual run around on foot - no real change there. The other mode of transport however is flight. Each player is equipped with a pair of wings (probably ought to make it a jetpack to be honest - although beating wings would look way more cool) that allows them to fly. The flight mode behaves in the following way.

  • Players have free mouse-look when flying.
  • Jump and crouch are used to gain and lose height.
  • Forwards, backwards, strafe left and strafe right act as thrusters in the direction they are pressing. These thrusters take a while for the player to get up to full speed (bit like having 50% air control really).
  • The flight mode can be activated and de-activated at will.
