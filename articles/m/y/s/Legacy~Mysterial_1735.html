Other Stuff

I write mods/programs for lots of other games. Most of them have been small, because anything large requires graphics, and I'm kinda afraid of starting/joining a mod group because it seems they have a higher chance of failing than marriages in America.

I suck at names. I don't know why.

I'm a big Linux fan. Wish Epic had given UT2003 a full Linux port (or at least had ported ucc make). On the other hand, it could've been worse *cough* Bioware *cough*. Sorry, had to do that. :D

I love random things, as you can see on my mods page. It probably comes from playing too much Doom 2 with the D!Zone program (which had an excellent and very customizable map item randomizer).

How I know I'm a hardcore programmer: When the Epic Bonus Pack for UT2003 came out, I found config variables in the Invasion code, located them in UT2003.ini, and posted the info to the official forums. An amused poster then replied, educating me to the presence of the menu controlling those options in Invasion's game rules tab, which I had missed because I had played online and then went straight to the code. :o
