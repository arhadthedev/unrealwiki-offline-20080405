From Unreal Wiki, The Unreal Engine Documentation Site

I first discovered UT in 2001 and played it all the time. After while I lost interest in playing against bots (I had AOL dial-up, and could never connect to online games) and shelved the game for a few years. Sometime in 2005 I regained interest when I discovered that you could *gasp* modify the game. Then in August I packed my bags for a year-long exchange trip to Latvia (where I am now). Unfortunately, I've had to put UT on hold again, because I haven't had any place to install it...:/ I have been working with C++ and Java a bit this year, keeping my mind fresh for college, where I'll study computer science. I can't wait for UT2007...it looks like it's going to rule all games yet produced :D I'm guessing I'll have to buy a new computer to be able to play it though...
