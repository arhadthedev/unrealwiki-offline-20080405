SuicideMissions{CLR}:Most Excellent TaoPaiPai, I plead with thee to show me mercy. Since the days of my youth, no fault could be found in me (oh, except for that first awful map). Hast thou ever found fault in me? Nay! for surely none could be found! Yet thou hast smitten my cursor, and kept it from selecting any surfaces.

Only one thing have I done, that might invoke thy wrath: Merge Polygons. Dost this thing anger thee? Shall not a man Merge Polygons? Forgive me, for I am a but a humble mapper strayed from the paths of righteousness. I pray thee, end the days of my sorrow, that I may pick myself up out of the sacloth and ashes, and serve thee in thy good favor again! Oh that thou wouldst bless me and my house with mapping leetness! Show now thy grace and kindness unto thy servant I pray!
