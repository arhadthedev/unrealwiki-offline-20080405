Discusssion

IceCreamYou: I figured this page should be created at some point. I suppose the Liandri Archives wiki was needed, but it seems to me that it overlaps with this one quite a lot.

MythOpus: I don't really a see a point on having that wiki either and I don't see why it should be advertised here. Just my two cents.

Wormbo: UnrealWiki is for developers, Liandri Archives are for players. The UnrealWiki didn't want player stuff before, so now we have a place to send them to.

Hal For the record, we did talk about it in the IRC channel (Mychaeel and tarquin were there, I think, and maybe a few others) before we began work on Liandri Archives. We all agreed that they would target different audiences. Liandri Archives will always stick to the Unreal series games from a players perspective. We even went to great lengths in choosing the name so that it would neither contain the name Unreal or Wiki. Why should it be advertised here? It needn't be, but we do appreciate the exposure. BeyondUnreal proudly hosts unrealwiki and we've made an additional effort to promote it via the network bar at the top of our front page. Additionally, we will link to unrealwiki in the appropriate spots on Liandri Archives. Hopefully, this will be mutually beneficial! :-)
